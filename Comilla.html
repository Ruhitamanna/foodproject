Matrivander rosmalai comilla

Each and every Bangladeshi people likes Roshmalai very much. It is very famous and mouthwatering sweetmeat item in our country. Matrivander at Monohorpur in the Comilla is the first shop where the Roshmalai was first made. At present, there are lots of sweetmeat shops in the Comilla and all over the country where Roshmalai is regularly made. But the Roshmalai of Matrivander, which is also known as Matrivander er Roshmalai is unique for its great taste. Proshiddho collect Roshmalai directly from the original Matrivander and provide home delivery service to the valuable customers. Proshiddho always ensure the best quality with the greatest taste.
